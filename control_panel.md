### Control Panel kya hoti hai?
Control Panel ek central hub hai aapke Windows computer ke liye jo aapko system settings ko manage karne mein help karta hai. Isse aap apne computer ke hardware, software, security, network, display aur bahut sari cheezen control kar sakte ho.
